Privacy Policy for SIFS

Last updated: 2025-10-23

This Privacy Policy describes how SIFS (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the SIFS Android application (“App”).

If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Camera and Photos

  • The App may request access to your camera (android.permission.CAMERA) to let you capture images/videos within the App.
  • Images and videos you capture are used only to provide the in‑app feature you initiated (e.g., attaching a photo to a record).

1.2 Files & Media

  • If you choose an image/video from your device, the App needs temporary access to that file. We do not scan or collect other media on your device.

1.3 Usage Data

  • We may collect basic, non‑identifying diagnostics (e.g., crash logs, performance). This helps us improve the App.

We do not sell personal information. We do not collect precise location unless explicitly requested for a feature.

2. How We Use Information

  • To enable features like capturing or attaching photos/videos.
  • To provide support and improve reliability and performance.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Service providers that process data on our behalf (e.g., analytics or crash reporting), under confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal reasons, if required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Data Storage & Retention

  • Media you capture may be uploaded to our servers only if required by the feature you use. Otherwise it remains on your device.
  • We retain uploaded content only as long as needed for the purpose it was collected or as required by law.

5. Your Choices

  • You can deny camera permission in Android settings. Some features may not work without it.
  • You can delete content you created inside the App where supported.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

7. Security

We apply reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8. International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. By using the App you consent to such transfers subject to applicable law.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted at https://example.com/privacy with a new “Last updated” date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or requests, contact us at support@example.com.
